Episode: #7  
April 13, 2020

Ether has some native mechanisms that contribute to its scarcity and value.
David and Ryan explore these value mechanisms and compare/contrast them with
money printer go brrrr.

This episode covers:

  * 1\. Scarcity mechanisms and fair games

  * 2\. Ether’s value mechanisms

  * 3\. How USD will fare vs. ETH
