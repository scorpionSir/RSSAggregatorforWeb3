### **Experienced legal hire bolsters Outlier Venture’s growing team**

![Jocelyn Roberts joins Outlier Ventures as Head of Legal Outlier
Ventures](https://outlierventures.io/wp-
content/uploads/2018/05/Jocelyn-1-1.jpg)

Outlier Ventures announced Jocelyn Roberts as Head of Legal, a newly created
position focused on supporting the firm as it continues to fund innovation in
new decentralised technologies and business models. Jocelyn joins from a
private equity platform where she served as Head of Legal after several years
in senior structuring roles at HSBC.

Stephan Apel, Managing Partner at Outlier Ventures commented:

> “ _With over 11 years of legal experience in corporate structuring at HSBC
> and securities law at Cleary Gottlieb, Jocelyn brings extensive cross-border
> experience in corporate and financial transactions to the team.”_

He continued:

> _“In a rapidly evolving regulatory environment, conflicting advice within
> and between specialist law firms, and public ambiguity from regulators,
> having a specialist in-house counsel dedicated to regulation and compliance
> is a powerful asset to continue Outlier Venture 's current momentum.”_

Jocelyn Roberts, Head of Legal at Outlier Ventures added:

> _“After working in the legal and financial sector for over a decade, it has
> become increasingly clear that there is extensive scope for innovation
> through decentralisation, in every industry. This is an area of fast-paced
> legal and regulatory development and I am looking forward to being part of a
> team that is shaping the landscape.”_

Outlier Ventures is actively working with policymakers and the wider industry
to further knowledge sharing on the evolution of decentralised technologies.
The company is engaged with the All-Party Parliamentary Group on Blockchain,
is a member of The Wall Street Blockchain Alliance and sits on Tech UK’s
distributed ledger working group.
